Welcome to Surah Mulk

Surah Mulk (The Sovereignty) is the 67th chapter of the Qur'an and holds immense significance. Comprising 30 beautiful Ayahs, it reflects on the magnificence of Allah's creation and His supreme authority over all that exists.

Listen to Surah Mulk

Benefits of Surah Mulk

Surah Mulk is widely known as the protector from the punishment of the grave. The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said:

"There is a Surah in the Qur'an which contains thirty Ayat that kept interceding for a man until his sins were forgiven. It is Surah Al-Mulk." (Tirmidhi)

Reciting this Surah every night before sleeping grants immense blessings and protection in both this life and the hereafter.
